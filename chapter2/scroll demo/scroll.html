Explore the rich history of FC Barcelona, which dates back to its foundation in 1899. Over the years, the club has achieved remarkable milestones and has become synonymous with excellence in football.

Barça played their first ever game
On December 8th 1899, Barça played their first ever game On the same day two years later, they played their first game with nets in the goals –a move that ensured there was no discussion about whether the ball had gone in or not!
FC Barcelona win European Cup
20 May 1992: FC Barcelona win European Cup Ronaldo Koeman's goal in the second half of extra time gave Barça victory over Sampdoria and their first ever European Cup trophy
